Adding Markers


A marker is a supported control button that lies within any custom checkbox element that is identified with the "marker" attribute. The checkbox plugin provide supports for either one or a maximum of two markers. We can define a single marker as shown below:

CheckBox (Single marker) 
<div checkbox>
  <div marker=""></div> <!-- marker -->
</div>
<input type="checkbox">

Similarly, we can also define double markers using the structure below:

CheckBox (Double markers) 
<div checkbox>
  <div marker=""></div>
  <div marker=""></div>
</div>
<input type="checkbox">

The double markers are mostly used for handling complex designed or animations. However, depending on the complexity or simplicity, a checkbox may or may not really require the use of markers. Let's take a closer look at a custom checkbox with a single marker on the next page.
